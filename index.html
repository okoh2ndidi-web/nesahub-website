Pricing (Stage-1)

Service Fee / Commission

NESAHub earns a small commission on completed jobs (commonly 5%–10%) depending on the category and job size. This helps us run verification, support, and tracking.

  • Client confirms scope & price first
  • Work begins after agreement
  • Receipts can be recorded

Fair costing model

We aim to reduce bargaining by using standard job categories, typical cost ranges, and clear material quotations.

  • Basic jobs can use minimum price guides
  • Complex jobs use inspection/assessment
  • Materials quotes are checked and receipted
